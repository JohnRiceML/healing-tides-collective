# Brand Guidelines — Healing Tides Collective

## Name
**Healing Tides Collective.** Working name; revisit after domain check (see `planning/setup-checklist.md`).

## Tagline candidates (test with founder)
1. The modern front door to wellness.
2. Find your fit.
3. Begin here.
4. Care, matched.
5. Less searching. More healing.

## Voice

| | We sound like | We don't sound like |
|---|---|---|
| Tone | Calm, grounded, certain | Hyped, clinical, woo-woo |
| Pacing | Spacious, deliberate | Punchy ad copy or dense paragraphs |
| Vocabulary | Plain, warm, specific | Jargon ("modalities", "synergy", "vibrations") |
| Emotion | Quietly reassuring | Enthusiastic, salesy |

**Litmus test:** read it aloud. If it sounds like a yoga teacher selling, rewrite. If it sounds like a doctor's intake form, rewrite.

## Voice in practice

| Persona | Tone shift | Sample headline |
|---|---|---|
| Overwhelmed Optimizer | Warm + clear | "We'll help you figure it out." |
| Practical Seeker | Direct + credible | "No guesswork. Just what works." |
| Curious Seeker | Supportive + relatable | "You don't have to figure this out alone." |

The page does not branch on persona. The default voice covers all three by leaning warm-and-clear, with the value prop section addressing each implicitly.

## Words we use
- Care, fit, match, guide, begin, find, the right, aligned, considered, thoughtful, grounded
- *Practitioner* over *provider* (warmer)
- *Care* over *services* or *treatment*
- *Begin* over *get started*

## Words we avoid
- Holistic *(overused — describe the thing instead)*
- Journey *(overused)*
- Wellness *(use sparingly — the category, not our promise)*
- Discover *(replace with* find *or* meet*)*
- Empower / unleash / transform *(salesy)*
- Modalities, synergy, optimize *(jargon)*

## Naming conventions (forward)
- Product surface: lowercase verbs in CTAs ("get matched", "begin")
- Section IDs / URLs: kebab-case
- Practitioner records: `practitioners` (not `providers`)
- Care types: `care-types` (not `services`, `modalities`)
