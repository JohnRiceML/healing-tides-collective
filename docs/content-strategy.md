# Content Strategy — Landing Page v1

Goal: convert a visitor into either an email signup (waitlist) or a "Get Matched" intent click. No directory, no resource hub on v1.

## Section-by-section structure

### 1. Hero
- **Headline (working):** "Find the care that fits."
  - Alt 1: "The modern front door to wellness."
  - Alt 2: "Begin here."
- **Subhead:** A guided way to find the right practitioner — therapy, acupuncture, reiki, movement, trauma-informed support, and more.
- **Primary CTA:** Get Matched
- **Secondary CTA (text link):** For practitioners →

### 2. Reassurance row (why we exist)
Three short statements addressing each persona without naming them.
- **No more endless browsing.** Tell us what you're looking for. We'll narrow it down.
- **Practitioners we trust.** Each one is curated for fit, not just listed.
- **No commitment.** Take the next step when you're ready.

### 3. How it works
Three steps, generous spacing, simple icons or numerals.
1. **Tell us a little.** A few questions about what you need.
2. **We match you.** Two or three practitioners, with the reasoning behind each.
3. **Reach out.** Connect directly when something feels right.

### 4. Care types we cover
A horizontal row or quiet grid. Not a directory — a signal of breadth.
- Therapy & counseling
- Acupuncture
- Reiki & energy work
- Movement-based care
- Trauma-informed support
- Bodywork
- Nutrition & functional medicine

(List subject to founder edit. Keep ≤8 items on v1.)

### 5. For practitioners
Split layout. Quiet pitch — this is not the page's main job, but practitioners do land here.
- **Headline:** Aligned referrals, less friction.
- **Body:** We match you with people who are actually a fit for your work. Less cold outreach, more meaningful first consultations.
- **CTA:** Join the practitioner network →

### 6. Email capture / waitlist
- **Headline:** Be the first to begin.
- **Subhead:** We're opening matches in waves. Drop your email and we'll let you know when it's your turn.
- **Field:** Email
- **CTA:** Notify me

### 7. Footer
Minimal. Logo + tagline. Contact email. Practitioner link. Social if/when they exist.

## Copy principles
- Short sentences. Often four words is enough.
- Headlines should sound like an exhale.
- Verbs over nouns. ("Find" beats "Discover the right.")
- One idea per section. If a section has two ideas, it's two sections.

## Out of scope for v1
- Practitioner profiles
- Reviews / testimonials (until we have real ones)
- Blog / resource library
- Insurance information
- Booking flow

## SEO notes (for v1)
- One H1 per page, in hero
- Meta title: "Healing Tides Collective — Find the care that fits"
- Meta description: derived from hero subhead
