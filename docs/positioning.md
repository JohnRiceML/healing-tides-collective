# Positioning

## What we are
- A **decision-making tool** for care
- A **bridge** between overwhelm and action
- A **modern front door** to wellness

## What we are NOT
- A directory (Psychology Today, Zocdoc, Mindbody)
- A content site / blog
- A booking platform (we hand off to practitioners)
- An insurance navigator
- A telehealth product

## The wedge
Every existing player optimizes for **breadth** — more practitioners, more filters, more reviews. Users finish more confused than they started.

We optimize for **clarity** — fewer, better matches with reasoning the user can trust. The product is a short guided flow, not a search box.

## Two-sided value

### For care seekers
- Stop browsing endless lists
- Get 2–3 matched practitioners, not 200
- Understand *why* each is a fit
- Feel guided, not gatekept

### For practitioners
- Receive aligned consultations, not cold leads
- Less time qualifying, more time treating
- Trust that referrals match their actual scope and approach
- Reduce admin and intake friction

## Competitive lens

| Competitor | What they do well | Our edge |
|---|---|---|
| Psychology Today | Massive directory, SEO-strong | Guided flow vs. search wall; matched output |
| Zocdoc | Booking infrastructure | We curate fit; we don't try to handle scheduling on day one |
| Mindbody | Holistic class booking | Different job — they're class discovery, we're care matching |
| Headway / Alma | Therapist matching + insurance | Therapy-only; we span clinical + holistic |
| BetterHelp | Easy onboarding, scale | Algorithmic match without curation; we're the considered alternative |

## The defensible moat (intent)
1. **Curated practitioner network** — we say no, which makes the matches mean something
2. **Match logic > search filters** — surfaces the *why* behind a recommendation
3. **Two-sided trust** — both seekers and practitioners feel respected by the experience
4. **Brand** — calm, considered, design-forward in a category that mostly looks the same

## What we'll say in one breath
> Healing Tides is a guided platform that matches you with the right care — clinical or holistic — across therapy, acupuncture, reiki, movement, and trauma-informed support. Less searching. More healing.
